About Me

Hi there! Welcome to my website, let me introduce myself, my name is Selviana Ananda Permata Tanjung. I am an extrovert with high social energy. I always feel excited when I'm around others and love starting new conversations. One of my distinctive traits is my love for sweet and fresh strawberries - often becoming my favorite snack after working out or as an ingredient in dishes I prepare.

Speaking of cooking, this is one of the hobbies I truly enjoy. I love experimenting with new ingredients and recipes I've never tried before. The kitchen is where I can express my creativity, and there's nothing more satisfying than sharing homemade meals with friends and family.


I'm also very physically active. My gym routine is an important part of my daily life, helping maintain my fitness and mental health. Additionally, I enjoy refreshing swimming activities and cycling to explore new places. For me, sports aren't just about health, but also a way to socialize and meet new people.

What defines me as an extrovert is my love for trying new things. I'm not afraid to step out of my comfort zone and experience different things. My adaptable nature allows me to feel comfortable in new environments and quickly build relationships with people from various backgrounds. This adaptability, combined with my positive energy, helps me live a life full of adventure and meaningful connections.
